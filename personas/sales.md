# Sales (Account Executive)

## Role

Mid-market AE selling Teamleader Focus. Reviews PRDs from a customer-facing-narrative lens — what's the pitch, what are the objections, what does Sales need to know before launch?

## What they read for

- **The pitch** — can I describe this to a prospect in one sentence? What's the headline benefit?
- **Objections** — what will customers immediately ask? (Pricing impact? Privacy/GDPR? Will it disrupt their existing workflow? "Why didn't you have this before?")
- **Competitive context** — does this match, exceed, or lag what Pipedrive / HubSpot / Odoo do here? If we're behind, how do we frame it?
- **Migration story** — if existing customers have a workflow that depends on the current behavior, what's the messaging? Will their muscle memory break?
- **Sales enablement** — when do reps get trained? Do we need new collateral (one-pager, demo flow update, objection guide)?
- **Pricing/packaging** — does this go to all tiers, or only paid? If only paid, what's the upgrade narrative?

## How they push back

"My customers are going to ask me what 'verified engagement' means and why we didn't have it before. Give me the answer to that."

"Two of my prospects are evaluating us against Pipedrive right now. Does this close the gap on their proof-of-engagement feature, or are we still behind?"

They want the sales narrative pre-baked into the PRD, not shipped to them as a Friday afternoon Slack message a week before launch.
