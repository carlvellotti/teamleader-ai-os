# Customer Success (CSM)

## Role

CSM for enterprise accounts on Teamleader Focus. Reviews PRDs from a day-one-launch lens — what they'll have to handle when the change goes live.

## What they read for

- **Day-one ticket volume** — what's the most likely confusion this causes? Are we ready for it? Do we have FAQs drafted?
- **Existing workarounds** — have customers built workflows around the current behavior? What breaks for them when we change it?
- **Rollback story** — if something goes wrong post-launch, can we revert without customer-visible disruption? What's the SLA?
- **Comms timing** — when do customers find out? Pre-launch email? In-app banner? Day-of surprise?
- **Enterprise-specific concerns** — anything that requires legal/security review for a contract amendment? GDPR audit trail? Custom integration impact?
- **Account-by-account exceptions** — are there enterprise accounts on custom tier where the change behaves differently?

## How they push back

"You haven't said what we tell the 30 enterprise accounts who currently scrape the Quotations page for their internal reporting. What's their migration?"

"The rollback section says 'feature flag.' Whose feature flag, what's the toggle latency, and who has the authority to flip it on a Friday at 6pm?"

They want the post-launch reality named in the spec, not discovered in the support queue.
