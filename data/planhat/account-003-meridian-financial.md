## 2026-04-08 — Risk & compliance review (annual)

Annual review with their head of compliance, Jean-Paul, and our solutions architect. Most of the conversation was about audit trail completeness, GDPR data handling, and the Cloudsign signature legal validity (which they confirmed is fine for their jurisdictions).

One issue surfaced: Jean-Paul's team had a recent case where they couldn't produce a complete activity log for a regulator audit, because the deal page had shown "Offline" for a quote that the customer had clearly opened (corroborated by their email server logs). The Cloudsign event log eventually had the open recorded, but the deal page signal didn't reflect it. The mismatch made the regulator ask follow-up questions.

For Meridian this is a mid-priority concern — they want the audit trail to be authoritative. They're not blocked but they noted it for renewal.

## 2026-03-15 — Earlier note from Jean-Paul

Mentioned in passing during a different call that their internal audit found three deals last year where the deal-page activity log conflicted with the Cloudsign event timestamps. They reconciled manually but it took time.
